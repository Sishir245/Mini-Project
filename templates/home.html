How to get Started?

Request an appointment

"Book an appointment through our website. you can fill one of our forms or click on "Book and Appointment"

Meeting

Attend the meeting at one of our offices or virtual rooms through Zoom or even a telephone call. Prepare all your Questions. You would get full information about our programs from the consultant.

Contract

After you agree on a course in a desired destination, you would sign a contact. In contract all responsibilities of all parties are mentioned.After the contract is signed we can proceed with your application.

Enrolment

All your Qualifications and required documents are assessed and translated and forwarded to the institute. If all pre requireements are met then a letter of offer is sent. The client should pay the fees and then a letter of acceptance is sent by the institute.

Visa

Visa application would be done by our visa specialists. All supporting document would be assessed and attached to the visa application.

Departure and Arrival of Packages

When the Visa is issued, we would prepare the appliciant for departure to the desired country.One of our consultants would meet the client at destination country to help with registration and settle down.
